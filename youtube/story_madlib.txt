The Brezza is available in manual, automatic and CNG options. 
The Kylaq does not offer a CNG trim. The Brezza starts from 
Rs 8.34 lakh to Rs 13.98 lakh, ex-showroom. The Maruti SUV 
gets seven solid colours and three dual tone. The single paint 
schemes are Pearl Midnight Black, Pearl Artic White, Splendid 
Silver, Magma Grey, Exuberant Blue, Brave Khaki and Sizzling Red. 
The dual tone colours are Sizzling Red with Midnight Black roof, 
Brave Khakhi with Artic White roof and Splendid Silver with 
Midnight Black roof.
